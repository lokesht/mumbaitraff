Registration of VIP Numbers

  • Under MMVR 1989 R54 the applications for new VIP nos. will be accepted from 5th February 2002 onwards during office hours.
  • On grant of the VIP nos the applicant shall pay the prescribed fees on the same day or on the following day during cash hours from 10:00 A.M. to 2:30 P.M.
  • The notified nos are available on payment of Rs.10000/- subject to availability. And the jumping no.s from current no. upto 1000 (except those notified) will be assigned on payment of Rs. 4000/- subject to availability.
  • If more than one application are received on one day, the nos will be allotted by lottery system by Dy. R.T.O. at 5.30 hrs on same day.
  • Reservation for such nos will be in force and will be valid for 30 days only.
  • All applications on plain paper will be accepted at R.T.O. main office by the concerned clerk of Non-Transport Series.
