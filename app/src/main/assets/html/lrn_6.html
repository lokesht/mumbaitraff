Unfitness to drive a Vehicle

The motor driving license cannot be issued to a person/applicant who is suffering from -
  • Defects of vision which cannot be corrected by spectacles.
  • Color or night blindness.
  • Hearing defects which cannot be corrected with the help of hearing aid.
  • Epilepsy.
  • Sudden attacks of loss of consciousness or giddiness.
  • Who has lost either hand or foot.
  • Suffer from any other disease or disability likely to cause danger to other road users. While driving is unfit to hold a driving license.
Miscellaneous
  • Change of address should be intimated within 30 days.
  • No person shall hold more than one smart card driving license.
