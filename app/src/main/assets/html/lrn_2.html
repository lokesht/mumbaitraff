Documents Required

The driving license is renewed with effect from the date of its expiry when the application is made before its expiry or within thirty days grace period. The driving license is renewed with effect from the date of renewal when the application is made after the grace period. The driving license cannot be renewed after the period of five years when it has ceased to be effective.

If the license is from other state it is desirable that the applicant should bring No Objection Certificate (N.O.C.) / confirmation of driving license from Original Licensing Authority.
